Volunteer for the Labour Party

We're glad you're interested in getting involved with the Labour Party in your area. Whether you're a member or just interested in helping out, we use this website to organise our campaign events and activists.

Click Start to tell us who are are and what you can do, and we'll be in touch. Before that though, here are some things you should be aware of:

  • This website is independent from the Labour Party, but it's run by committed and experienced Labour Party members.

  • We'll store your information and we'll pass it on to members of the Labour Party who'll use it to let you know about events and campaigns.

  • If you ask us to, we'll send you an automated email every so often with news and information about events in your area.

  • We may also pass on the information you provide to the Labour party to help with their candidate selections and to help find supporters to sign candidates' nomination forms.

The main purpose of this website is to track attendance at events, let people know what events are happening, and monitor how much campaigning is being done.

If you want to help us out, click Start.

For more information on how we'll use your data, have a look at our Data protection statement.
